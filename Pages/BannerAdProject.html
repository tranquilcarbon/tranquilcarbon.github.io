The Banner ad Project

What are banner ads?

The banner ad is a rectangular advert able to be placed on the top or bottom of a website. The most common form is the 468x60 Veriant which is what the ad type is named after, but there are also many more.


The banner ad project aims to create a easily searchable advert database, from the early 90s to the early 2000s.
